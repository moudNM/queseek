      Point System

      Quests:
      Hero Quests: (20 coins per post) -Limited to 3 posts of lost item(s) per user
      Side Quests: (5 coins)- Eg: Errands such as printing notes / Collab
      There are three levels of quests and this will affect the order the Quests are displayed on the Quest Home page. All quests are posted based on the time it was submitted (i.e. Most recent to older posts). The level 3 will be displayed at the top while level 2 and level 1 below it.
      *Level is dependent on the user who posts the quests. For Level 2 and 3 the user who post the quests will need to contribute their points according to the surplus of the level. Example:
      Level 1 - 20 coins from system
      Level 2 - 20 coins from system and 10 coins from the user.
      Level 3 - 20 coins from system and 20 coins from the user.
      Featured Quests:
      (Additional 5 coins on top of the existing points for the quests)
      • Only limited to users based on the Top 20% weekly coins ranking.
      • Each user can only attempt up to 3 featured quests in a week.
      • First 5 users to complete the featured quest will get 5 coins, the next 5 will get 0.5x (2.5 coins)
      Bonus points:
      Uses who managed to hit these goals will get additional points.

      Seekers

      What can I do with the coins?
      Users can use the accumulated coins that they received from completing the quests for the following purpose:
      • The coins earned will help the user to level up. At a higher level, the user will have access to more quests.
      • They can unlock a new avatar.
      • The points can be used to change the level of the quests that the users posted.
      Where can I check the quests that I have accepted?
      Seekers can go to their profile page and under it will show a list of quests that they have previously accepted.
      How should I verify the lost item(s) with the rightful owner?
      In the event of a meetup, the rightful owner should either have a prove of identification that the item belongs to him/her or the description of the lost item(s) provided by the seekers should accurately matched with the information given by the owner of lost item(s).
      How do I earn coins?
      Users can earn points by completing the different types of quests. Users can also earn point by sharing the post. Refer to the above-mentioned point systems to know the full details on how the points are rewarded.
      What is a Leaderboard?
      Ranking
      • The weekly ranking is only for the top 20 users where the user who accumulates the most coins will be given the title of “Grandmaster” and the rest if users will be given the title of “Treasure hunters”.
      • The monthly ranking is for the top 50 users (and the same goes with the weekly ranking)
      • All-time titles: Refer to the Badge System below
      Badge System
      Users can earn points by completing the different types of quests. Users can also earn point by sharing the post. Refer to the above-mentioned point systems to know the full details on how the points are rewarded.

      General

      What is QueSeek?
      Queseek is a platform that builds a community of people by completing different types of quests together.
      What are the different types of quests and seeks?
      There are two types of quests: Hero Quests and Side Quests.
      Hero Quests are quest posted by people who have reported their item as lost.
      Side Quests are interactive mini-games that users can take part in to earn some bonus points. The two type of side quests are Collaborative based and Community based.
      • Collaborative Based: Companies & Administrators posts quests such as finding QR code around campus.
      • Community Based: Helping other students to run errands in school such as printing lecture notes or buying takeaways.

      Seek are the list of founded items that have been reported to the system.
      What are featured quests?
      They are quests that have been featured by the moderators of Queseek. These quests are usually available for a limited time only as a collaborations with other organisation or for an event. Featured quests will have bonus points available for uses to earn.
      I have lost my belonging, what should I do?
      Firstly, users can browse through the Seek reported to see if their item have already been found by others are are already reported on the website. If the user can’t find any listing of their item, they can post a Quest report.
      Simply click on the “Report your lost item” button on the right hand side of the page to submit a report.
      I found a item in school, what should I do?
      Simply click on the “Report a seek” button on our home page, fill up the form and submit it! Fill up the basic information of the item you found and double check with the owner for the specific information such as “What cards are in the wallet?” before returning it to the owner. If you are not available to meet up with the owner, you may pass the item to the security counter of the school.
